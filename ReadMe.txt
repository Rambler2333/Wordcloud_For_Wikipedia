Wikipedia Word Cloud 

+ ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This project may help you guys who just learned some concepts but are unaware of the topics professionals are usually talking about. The word cloud applied pre-trained NLP model (spacy package) will give to a visual image including all words around the certain topic or term you may want to explore further. The larger the front the more frequently the words appear in the article.

This project is still in the developing stage, pls feel free to leave any suggestions and comments!
